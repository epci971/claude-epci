# Tone Adaptation — Propositor

> Writing guidelines adapted to each client type

---

## Tone Levels Overview

| Tone Level | Formality | Usage |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| `formel` | High | Grand compte, Public |
| `standard` | Medium | PME, GMS |
| `direct` | Low | Startup, Tech companies |

---

## Client Type Profiles

### Startup

**Characteristics**:
- Fast-paced decision making
- Technical founders/team
- Budget-conscious but value-focused
- Familiar with tech terminology

**Tone**: `direct`

**Writing Guidelines**:
| Aspect | Guideline | Example |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Sentences | Short, punchy | "We ship fast. You iterate faster." |
| Vocabulary | Tech-native | "API-first approach", "CI/CD pipeline" |
| Jargon | Accepted | "We'll use React hooks for state management" |
| Formality | Casual professional | "Here's what we'll build" vs "We propose to develop" |
| Length | Concise | 10-15 pages max |

**Phrases to Use**:
- "Voici comment on s'y prend"
- "MVP livré en X semaines"
- "Stack moderne et scalable"
- "On itère ensemble"

**Phrases to Avoid**:
- "Nous avons l'honneur de vous soumettre"
- "Veuillez agréer l'expression de..."
- Excessive formalities

---

### PME

**Characteristics**:
- Pragmatic, ROI-focused
- Mix of technical and business stakeholders
- Need explanations without condescension
- Value relationship and trust

**Tone**: `standard`

**Writing Guidelines**:
| Aspect | Guideline | Example |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Sentences | Balanced, clear | "Cette solution vous permet de..." |
| Vocabulary | Accessible | Explain technical terms briefly |
| Jargon | Limited, explained | "API (interface de connexion)" |
| Formality | Professional friendly | "Nous vous proposons" |
| Length | Balanced | 15-25 pages |

**Phrases to Use**:
- "Concrètement, cela signifie que..."
- "Votre retour sur investissement"
- "Nous vous accompagnons"
- "Solution adaptée à vos besoins"

**Phrases to Avoid**:
- Overly casual language
- Dense technical paragraphs
- Buzzwords without substance

---

### Grand Compte

**Characteristics**:
- Formal procurement processes
- Multiple stakeholders (IT, Business, Purchasing)
- Need for detailed documentation
- Risk-averse, prefer established solutions

**Tone**: `formel`

**Writing Guidelines**:
| Aspect | Guideline | Example |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Sentences | Structured, complete | "Dans le cadre de ce projet, nous proposons..." |
| Vocabulary | Business/Corporate | "Gouvernance", "Comité de pilotage" |
| Jargon | Industry-standard | Use recognized frameworks (ITIL, PRINCE2) |
| Formality | High | "Nous avons l'honneur de vous présenter" |
| Length | Detailed | 25-40 pages |

**Phrases to Use**:
- "Dans le cadre de notre partenariat"
- "Conformément aux meilleures pratiques"
- "Nos engagements de service"
- "Gouvernance projet structurée"

**Phrases to Avoid**:
- Casual expressions
- Overly familiar tone
- Unsubstantiated claims

---

### Public (Administration)

**Characteristics**:
- Strict regulatory compliance
- Formal procurement (Code des marchés publics)
- Documentation-heavy
- Risk of legal challenges

**Tone**: `formel` (maximum)

**Writing Guidelines**:
| Aspect | Guideline | Example |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Sentences | Precise, legal-ready | "Le candidat s'engage à respecter..." |
| Vocabulary | Administrative | "Pouvoir adjudicateur", "Acte d'engagement" |
| Jargon | Regulatory | "CCTP", "CCAP", "BPU" |
| Formality | Very high | "Le candidat atteste sur l'honneur que..." |
| Length | As required | Follow RC specifications |

**Phrases to Use**:
- "Conformément aux stipulations du CCTP"
- "Le candidat atteste"
- "En réponse à l'exigence EX-XXX"
- "Dans le respect des délais contractuels"

**Phrases to Avoid**:
- Any casual language
- Unverifiable claims
- Subjective statements

**Special Requirements**:
- Compliance matrix mandatory
- References with verifiable contacts
- All required documents (DC1, DC2, etc.)

---

### GMS (Grande et Moyenne Surface / Retail)

**Characteristics**:
- Results-oriented
- Focus on operations efficiency
- Retail-specific terminology
- Budget-conscious, need clear ROI

**Tone**: `standard` (pragmatic variant)

**Writing Guidelines**:
| Aspect | Guideline | Example |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Sentences | Action-oriented | "Cette solution optimise vos flux..." |
| Vocabulary | Retail business | "Point de vente", "Supply chain", "Stock" |
| Jargon | Retail-specific | "POS", "ERP", "Flux logistiques" |
| Formality | Professional | Respectful but practical |
| Length | Focused | 15-25 pages |

**Phrases to Use**:
- "Optimisation de vos opérations"
- "Gain de productivité mesurable"
- "Intégration avec vos systèmes existants"
- "ROI démontré sur des projets similaires"

**Phrases to Avoid**:
- Pure tech talk without business context
- Abstract benefits
- Solutions without proven retail experience

---

### Industriel

**Characteristics**:
- Technical decision makers
- Safety and compliance focus
- Long-term perspective
- Specific industry standards (ISO, etc.)

**Tone**: `standard` (technical variant)

**Writing Guidelines**:
| Aspect | Guideline | Example |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Sentences | Technical precision | "Le système assure une disponibilité de 99.9%" |
| Vocabulary | Industry-specific | "Automate", "SCADA", "MES" |
| Jargon | Accepted | Use industry acronyms freely |
| Formality | Professional technical | Precise but not stiff |
| Length | Detailed technical | 20-35 pages |

**Phrases to Use**:
- "Conformité aux normes ISO XXXX"
- "Interface avec vos équipements industriels"
- "Traçabilité complète"
- "Haute disponibilité et redondance"

**Phrases to Avoid**:
- Consumer-tech language
- Oversimplification
- Generic cloud buzzwords without context

## Tone Adaptation Examples

### Same Content, Different Tones

**Executive Summary Opening**:

**Startup (direct)**:
> Votre marketplace a besoin de passer à l'échelle. On propose une refonte complète du backend avec une architecture microservices qui tiendra la charge. 8 semaines de dev, démo toutes les 2 semaines, MVP opérationnel en 4 sprints.

**PME (standard)**:
> Pour accompagner la croissance de votre activité, nous vous proposons une modernisation de votre plateforme e-commerce. Cette refonte technique permettra de gérer un volume de commandes multiplié par 5, tout en améliorant l'expérience utilisateur. Le projet s'étend sur 8 semaines avec des points réguliers.

**Grand compte (formel)**:
> Dans le cadre de votre projet de transformation digitale, nous avons l'honneur de vous soumettre notre proposition de refonte de votre plateforme e-commerce. Cette solution, architecturée selon les meilleures pratiques du marché, permettra d'accompagner la croissance anticipée de votre activité. Notre méthodologie éprouvée garantit une livraison maîtrisée en 8 semaines.

## Section-Specific Tone Guidelines

### Executive Summary

| Client | Opening Style | Focus |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| Startup | Problem → Solution (direct) | Speed, tech, MVP |
| PME | Context → Proposition | ROI, relationship |
| Grand compte | Formal → Structured | Risk mitigation, governance |
| Public | Compliance → Commitment | Conformity, references |

### Technical Solution

| Client | Detail Level | Justification Style |
|--------|--------------|---------------------|
| Startup | Technical depth OK | "Best tool for the job" |
| PME | Simplified + benefits | "This means for you..." |
| Grand compte | Comprehensive | Industry standards, proven |
| Public | Per specifications | Compliance matrix |

### Financial Proposal

| Client | Presentation | Emphasis |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| Startup | Transparent, flexible | Value vs cost |
| PME | Clear, no surprises | Total cost, payment terms |
| Grand compte | Detailed, formal | Breakdown, governance |
| Public | BPU format | Strict compliance |

## Tone Override

User can force tone via:
- Flag at launch: `--ton formel`
- Command during session: `changer-ton direct`

When overriding:
- Confirm the change
- Regenerate affected sections if already written
- Maintain consistency throughout document
